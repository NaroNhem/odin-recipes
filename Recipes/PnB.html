Descirption

Yummy easy to put together snack. Simple and clean. Extremely versatile

Ingredients

  • Choice of bread
  • Choice of nut bread
  • Choice of fruit spread or honey

Steps

  1. Grab 2 pieces of bread and lay them side by side
  2. On slice of bread, add peanute butter
  3. On the other slice of bread, add your spread
  4. Combine the two slices together so that the sides with spread on it touch
